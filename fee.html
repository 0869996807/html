PRIMARY

The Tuition fees for june and the first term fees must be paid before the en of May. This applies even to those who are seeking financial assistance from the school. Those in receipt of Government Concessions are promoted to the next higher class need not pay fees.

Fess will be paid thorugh the South Indian Bank. Fee bills will be available online which parents can print form the website and pay to the bank by cash.

Whewe possible, parents are requested to pay fees in advance for six months or a year. If the fees for the month are not paid before 10th of the month, the boy is liable to be sent home. If fees cannot be paid in time the Principal's or Vice Principal's permission must be obtained in writng. Please state in your application when you will be able to pay the fees. If the fees, are not paid within 2 monthhs the name will be struck off the rolls
